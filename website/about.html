About the Game

Discover the lore, gameplay mechanics, and technical foundation of Cosmic Survivors - the ultimate bullet heaven experience in deep space.

The Story

The universe's last hope against an endless alien invasion

You are a lone survivor in the depths of uncharted space, piloting a highly advanced combat vessel. An ancient alien force has awakened, and waves of hostile creatures pour through dimensional rifts, threatening all life in the galaxy.

Your ship is equipped with experimental auto-targeting weapon systems that fire continuously at nearby threats. As you survive longer, you unlock new weapons and abilities, each more devastating than the last.

There is no escape. No reinforcements. Only survival. How long can you hold out against the endless cosmic swarm?

Game Features

10 Unique Weapons

Projectile

Plasma Laser

Rapid-fire homing laser beams that track enemies. High DPS, perfect for clearing swarms.

Spread

Shotgun Burst

Wide spread of energy pellets. Devastating at close range, melts single targets.

Homing

Guided Missiles

Smart missiles that chase the nearest enemy. Excellent for crowd control and guaranteed hits.

Orbital

Electron Orbit

Rotating energy spheres that orbit your ship. Passive defense that damages on contact.

Area

Black Hole Generator

Creates miniature singularities that pull enemies in and deal massive AOE damage over time.

Beam

Particle Beam

Continuous energy beam that pierces through multiple enemies. High range and penetration.

Explosive

Cluster Bombs

Lobbed projectiles that explode into smaller bomblets. Massive area damage potential.

Lightning

Chain Lightning

Electric bolts that jump between enemies. Incredible for packed enemy groups.

Melee

Energy Blade

Spinning energy blade around your ship. High damage, no cooldown, close-range dominance.

Drone

Combat Drones

Deploy autonomous drones that follow and attack independently. Increases firepower significantly.

10 Enemy Types

Swarm

Scout Drones

Fast, fragile, and numerous. Overwhelm with sheer numbers. Low HP, high speed.

Charger

Assault Rams

Charge directly at your ship with high speed. Medium HP, predictable but dangerous in groups.

Teleporter

Phase Wraiths

Blink around the battlefield, appearing near you. Disruptive and hard to track.

Splitter

Hivemind Spawners

Split into smaller enemies upon death. Can quickly overwhelm if not handled efficiently.

Support

Shield Healers

Restore health to nearby enemies. Priority targets that must be eliminated first.

Sniper

Attack from afar with slow but powerful projectiles. Stay mobile to avoid their shots.

Armored Juggernauts

Massive HP pools and slow movement. Require sustained fire to take down.

Elite

Commander Units

Kamikaze
