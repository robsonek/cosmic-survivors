Game Mechanics

Deep systems that create emergent gameplay

Combo Chain System

Chain kills within 2 seconds to build devastating multipliers and unlock milestone bonuses.

How It Works

  • Kill an enemy to start a combo chain
  • Each subsequent kill within 2 seconds extends the combo
  • Multiplier increases from 1x to 10x
  • Missing the window resets your combo to zero
  • Higher multipliers apply to XP gain from each kill

Endless Scaling

After 20 minutes in any difficulty, the game enters "Endless Mode":

  • Enemy HP and damage increase by +5% per minute
  • Enemy spawn rate increases by +3% per minute
  • Boss health scales by +10% per minute
  • Maximum enemy count increases by +10 per minute
  • No upper limit - survive as long as you can!

Progression Systems

Multiple layers of growth ensure every run makes you stronger, both within the session and across your entire account.

XP & Leveling

Gain experience from killing enemies and reach new levels to unlock upgrade choices.

  • XP required per level scales at level^1.15
  • Each level grants choice of 3 random upgrades
  • Higher combo multipliers increase XP gain up to 10x
  • XP orbs are automatically collected after 1 second near player
  • Magnet power-up or passive increases collection radius by 3x
