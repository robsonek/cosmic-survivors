Build Guides

Build Philosophy

In Cosmic Survivors, your build is everything. The right combination of weapons and passives can transform you from a struggling survivor into an unstoppable force. Each build has its own playstyle, strengths, and weaknesses.

Key principles:

  • Synergize weapon types with complementary passives
  • Balance offensive power with survivability based on difficulty
  • Adapt your strategy to the wave composition
  • Prioritize weapon evolutions that match your playstyle
  • Don't ignore movement - positioning is survival
