FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Cosmic Survivors

What is Cosmic Survivors? ‚ñº
Cosmic Survivors is a bullet heaven roguelite game set in deep space. Survive endless waves of alien enemies, collect powerful weapons, level up, and evolve your arsenal. It combines the addictive gameplay of survivor-like games with multiplayer co-op support for up to 4 players.
Is it free to play? ‚ñº
Yes! Cosmic Survivors is completely free to play directly in your web browser. No downloads, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. Just jump in and start surviving.
What platforms is it available on? ‚ñº
Currently, Cosmic Survivors is available on web browsers for desktop. The game runs on modern browsers with WebGPU or WebGL2 support. Mobile platform support is planned for future updates.
How long is a typical run? ‚ñº
A typical run lasts between 15-30 minutes, depending on your skill level and the difficulty mode selected. Nightmare mode runs can extend longer as you face increasingly challenging waves. Boss encounters occur every 5 waves, providing major milestones throughout your run.
Is there a story? ‚ñº
Cosmic Survivors features a light narrative about a lone survivor stranded in deep space, fighting against endless waves of hostile alien forces. The focus is on gameplay, but environmental storytelling and achievement descriptions expand the universe.
How many players can play together? ‚ñº
Up to 4 players can team up in real-time co-op multiplayer. Form a squad with your friends and combine your weapons and abilities to survive longer against the alien hordes. The difficulty scales automatically based on player count.
How do weapons work? ‚ñº
Weapons in Cosmic Survivors auto-fire at the nearest enemy, allowing you to focus on movement and positioning. Each weapon has unique stats (damage, cooldown, range) and can be upgraded multiple times. You can equip up to 6 weapons simultaneously, creating powerful synergies.
What are weapon evolutions? ‚ñº
Weapon evolutions are powerful transformations that occur when you combine a max-level weapon with a specific passive ability. For example, the Magic Wand at max level combined with the Lucky passive evolves into the Holy Wand, which shoots multiple homing projectiles with increased damage.
How does the combo system work? ‚ñº
Kill enemies within a 2-second window to build combos. The multiplier ranges from 1x to 10x, increasing XP gained and gold drops. Combos reset if you don't get a kill within the time window, so keep the momentum going! Elite kills grant bonus combo time.
What are Elite enemies? ‚ñº
Elite enemies have a 5% spawn chance and come with special modifiers like Vampiric (heals on hit), Fast (increased movement speed), Tanky (extra health), or Explosive (damages nearby enemies on death). They glow with a distinctive aura and reward 3x normal XP when defeated.
How do I unlock Nightmare mode? ‚ñº
Survive for 15 minutes on Normal difficulty to unlock Nightmare mode. This brutal difficulty features 50% more enemies, bosses have double health, and new enemy variants appear. It's the ultimate test of your skills and build optimization.
What are environmental hazards? ‚ñº
Starting from wave 10, environmental hazards begin appearing: meteor showers that create explosion zones, electric storms that form damage fields, and cosmic anomalies that pull or push entities. These hazards affect both you and enemies, adding another layer of tactical depth.
How many passive abilities are there? ‚ñº
There are 8 passive abilities: Thorns (reflect damage), Life Steal (heal on kill), Lucky (critical chance), Berserker (damage scales with missing health), Guardian Angel (revive once), Momentum (speed increases with kills), Glass Cannon (more damage, less health), and Area Master (increased AoE size).
What engine is used? ‚ñº
Cosmic Survivors is built with Phaser 4 for rendering and game engine functionality, combined with bitECS for high-performance Entity Component System architecture. This allows the game to handle 1000+ entities simultaneously while maintaining 60 FPS.
What are the system requirements? ‚ñº
You need a modern web browser with WebGPU or WebGL2 support. Recommended: Chrome 94+, Firefox 93+, Edge 94+, or Safari 15+. A dual-core processor and 4GB RAM should be sufficient. The game automatically adjusts quality settings based on your hardware performance.
Can I play on mobile? ‚ñº
Desktop browsers are recommended for the best experience. Mobile optimization and touch controls are planned for future updates. The game currently requires mouse/keyboard or gamepad input for optimal gameplay.
How is performance optimized? ‚ñº
The game targets 60 FPS using several optimization techniques: spatial hash grid for collision detection (64px cells), object pooling for projectiles and particles, ECS architecture for cache-friendly data access, and automatic quality scaling. The engine can handle over 1000 enemies on screen simultaneously.
Is my progress saved? ‚ñº
Yes! Your meta-progression (talent points, achievements, unlocks) is automatically saved using browser localStorage. If you create an account, progress is also backed up to the cloud via Nakama servers, allowing you to play across different devices.
What languages are supported? ‚ñº
Currently, Cosmic Survivors is available in English. Full localization support for multiple languages (Spanish, French, German, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean) is planned for a future update using the i18next framework.
How does multiplayer work? ‚ñº
Multiplayer uses real-time co-op via Nakama open-source server, combining WebSocket for game state and WebRTC for low-latency communication. Host creates a lobby, shares a join code, and up to 3 friends can join for 4-player co-op action.
Is there cross-platform play? ‚ñº
Yes! Since Cosmic Survivors runs in web browsers, all players on supported browsers can play together regardless of operating system (Windows, Mac, Linux). As long as you have a compatible browser, you can join any multiplayer session.
What about latency and lag? ‚ñº
The game uses client-side prediction to make your local inputs feel responsive, combined with a 100ms interpolation buffer to smooth out other players' movements. Server reconciliation corrects any prediction errors. Most players with stable connections under 150ms ping will have a smooth experience.
Can I play solo? ‚ñº
Absolutely! Single-player is the default mode. You can enjoy the full game experience solo, and all progression, achievements, and unlocks work identically in both single-player and multiplayer modes.
Is there PvP mode? ‚ñº
Not currently. Cosmic Survivors focuses on co-operative PvE gameplay where players work together against AI enemies. PvP modes may be considered for future updates based on community feedback.
