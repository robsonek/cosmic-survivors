v0.6.0 "Content Explosion" LATEST Feb 5, 2026

Massive content update with 20 parallel agents adding extensive features across all systems.

  • Weapons: 5 new weapons added - Lightning Chain, Orbital Blades, Missile Swarm, Freeze Ray, Black Hole
  • Enemies: 5 new enemy types - Splitter, Teleporter, Shielder, Exploder, Healer
  • Combo System: New multiplier system (1-10x) with visual feedback and decay
  • Kill Streak: 5-tier reward system (Hot Streak, Rampage, Unstoppable, Godlike, Legendary)
  • Power-Ups: 6 collectible types - Damage Boost, Speed Boost, Shield, Freeze Time, Magnet, Multi-Shot
  • Treasure Chests: Bronze/Silver/Gold chests with scaling rewards
  • Environmental Hazards: 5 types after wave 10 - Asteroids, Black Holes, Solar Flares, Ion Storms, Wormholes
  • Boss Patterns: 5 attack patterns - Bullet Hell, Laser Sweep, Summon Minions, Ground Slam, Enrage
  • Elite Modifiers: 5 elite variants - Vampiric, Fast, Giant, Shielded, Explosive
  • Weapon Evolution: 3-stage evolution system with unique upgrades per weapon
  • MiniMap: Radar system showing enemies, pickups, and hazards
  • Damage Numbers: Comic-style floating damage text with critical indicators
  • Screen Effects: Hit stop, slow motion, chromatic aberration, vignette
  • Trail Effects: Afterimage trails and glow effects for player/projectiles
  • Wave Announcer: Visual/audio announcements for wave starts and boss spawns
  • Achievements: 10 new achievements with popup notifications
  • Stats Screen: End-game statistics with DPS graph visualization
  • Difficulty Modes: Easy, Normal, and Nightmare modes with scaling modifiers
  • Passive Abilities: 8 new passive upgrades in talent pool
  • Audio: SFX trigger system integrated with all new features
  • Bug Fix: Fixed vignette effect persisting on Game Over screen
